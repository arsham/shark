*search_tips*

Chaining search:
/term1/;/term2/;/term3


gv                     : re-highlight the previous highlighted lines from somewhere else.
:noh                   : clear the search highlights


:%s /foo/bar/g         : Change "foo" to "bar" on every line
:s /foo/bar/g          : Change "foo" to "bar" on just the current line
